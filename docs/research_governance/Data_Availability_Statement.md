# Data Availability Statement

The study uses public organisational records and public manufacturer website evidence to construct and evaluate the Regional Manufacturing Capability Taxonomy (RMCT) as a public capability observability framework. The regional company frame was derived from publicly available Companies House records and manufacturing-related SIC codes. Public manufacturer websites were crawled between 2026-06-03 18:23 and 2026-06-04 10:05, based on the available scrape metadata.

The full raw and cleaned website texts are not redistributed with this article or in the public repository. Although the websites were publicly accessible at the time of collection, the full text remains subject to third-party website copyright, changing website terms, and possible removal or revision by the source organisations. To support reproducibility while limiting redistribution of third-party text, the public repository provides derived and provenance-oriented data only, including anonymised company identifiers, SIC codes, regional folders, crawl-date fields where available, website text status, document hashes, controlled vocabulary terms, anonymised firm-level RMCT profiles, benchmark briefs, aggregate benchmark results, and expert-validation templates.

Short extracted evidence spans may be shared where required for validation of assigned RMCT labels, but these are limited to the minimum text necessary to support audit and expert review. Personal names, email addresses, telephone numbers and similar contact details were removed during preprocessing where detected and are not intentionally shared in the released data. The released files do not include full raw website pages or full cleaned website text.

The public reproducibility package is available at: https://github.com/zhizhi811001/RMCT-public-data-capability-classification. The repository contains the controlled vocabulary, anonymised company profiles, document hashes, benchmark inputs and aggregate outputs, expert-validation protocol and sample template, and reproducibility documentation. Users who wish to reproduce the full website-text extraction must re-crawl the original public sources or use their own authorised local website corpus, because the original third-party website texts are not redistributed.

Companies House data are available from Companies House under its applicable public-data terms. Manufacturer website content remains controlled by the respective website owners. Absence of a capability term in the released data should be interpreted as absence of public evidence in the processed corpus, not as absence of the underlying operational capability.
